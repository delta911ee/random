How to play?

Welcome to HoverGuess. Your objective is to guess a secret word by hovering over all letters and finding out whether the secret word contains the letter or not. If the secret word contains a particular letter, it will change it's color upon hovering over it. If you are still clueless after finding the letters, you can use the hint button to get a hint. You have a total of 5 tries to correctly guess the word.
